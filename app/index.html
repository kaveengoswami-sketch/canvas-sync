Canvas Sync

Enter your info, pick your courses, route them to Todoist projects, connect GitHub — then it runs itself in the cloud, 3× a day, even with your laptop off.

1 · GitHub checking…

GitHub runs your sync for free, around the clock. Connect once.

2 · Notifications & timezone

Load projects, then route each course to one below. Install the ntfy app and subscribe to your topic to receive pushes.

3 · Run schedule

When the sync runs each day, in your local timezone (set above). GitHub converts to UTC for you.

Runs may be a few minutes late; daylight-saving can shift them ~1h.

4 · Schools & courses

Add each Canvas account, fetch courses, tick what to track, and pick its Todoist project.
